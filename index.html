About Me

I started programming in 2019 to satisfy a degree requirement. I was nervous to take the course because I had absolutely no background in computer science at the time and programming looked far too difficult. When I started the class, though, it became my new favorite hobby, and in 2021 I decided to pursue software development full-time.

My ultimate goal is to use my background in mathematics to create software that benefits humanity and solves the most complex problems. I’d also like to learn more about embedded systems and machine learning, and I’d like the freedom to continuously improve my developing capabilities.
